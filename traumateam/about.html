Trauma-Squad and Equipment

Each Trauma Team squad consists of: An Aerodyne pilot and co-pilot, 2 Security Specialists, Lead Emergency Medical Technician and an EMT Assistant.

Both the Squad and it's AV are equipped with custom-made weapon systems for non-lethal and lethal capabilities to remove ANY obstacles between the client and safety.

Each squad travels with a heavily armoured AV-4 supported by mobile tanker refuelling stations to ensure extraction from land, air or sea.

The AV houses the most sophisticated revivification and life support technology available, anything from rapid surgery kits, adaptive nanosymbiosis, regenerators, to a mobile cryotank.
