Memberships and Services

All plans include the TraumaMonitor™ cyberware

(TraumaStick™ available for those without cyberware)

Golden Plan

From the moment Trauma Team is dispatched, the customer also has to pay 100cr/minute until arrival at the nearest hospital. Customer must cover the cost of all ammunition that was fired, all of the fuel that was used, and any equipment or personnel damage.

Customer receives 50% off, if the Trauma Team is not able to reach the customer within 7 minutes.

[price: 2,000cr/month or 21,500cr/year]

Platinum Plan

Guaranteed response time of 5 minutes. The customer is taken to a medical center upon pickup. Customer must cover the cost of all ammunition that was fired, all of the fuel that was used, and any equipment or personnel damage.

Customer receives money back, if the Trauma Team is not able to reach the customer within the guaranteed 5 minutes.

[price: 12,000cr/month or 130,000cr/year]

Diamond Plan

The response time for this plan is 3 minutes, fastest in the world. Once the customer is picked up, they will be taken to the state of the art Trauma Team Center. All ammunition and fuel costs are covered, but the customer has to pay for any equipment or personnel damage.

[price: 36,000cr/month or 420cr/year]

TraumaStick™

For most cases, customers will be fitted with the TraumaMonitor™ cyberware, but this is the alternative in case additional cyberware is not desirable. Trauma Team will be dispatched if TraumaStick is broken.
