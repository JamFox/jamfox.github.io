Mega-sized PaXXXXima Mall Soon Open!

Chloë Alex 10:25 AM, February 01, 2080

You've all seen PaXima grocery stores around Night City, but a one of this size? Night City's own PaXXXXima megamall will be just one X short of five - their biggest mall so far on Crystal Palace. The brand new PaXima megamall will open in Wellsprings on the February 10th with a party and a sale: 20% off everything for two days!

The widely known worldwide franchise carries an impressively wide catalogue of products. Chances are, if you can think of it, they will have it. From diapers and synthsnacks to cyberware and smart-rifles. Like their slogan says: "Have it all!"

Allan Remmel, management board member of PaXima said:

“We shall not build a dull and mediocre box. We shall create something fabulous, something previously unseen around Night City. Something so great that it sets a new standard hardly reachable for others. This is how I have been taught to think in PaXima since 2015, and 65 years later those very principles are underlying the construction of PaXXXXima of Night City. Experts of our field will flock to the megamall, they will be coming to see Night City from a new angle.“

The mall will also feature fabulous views to the Orbital Air Space Center, so you can take a break from shopping to watch rocket launches. In addition there will be a Braindance club, restaurants and plenty of space to relax in the glass covered outside, built on top of carways. You will also be able to access the NCART system directly from the mall!
