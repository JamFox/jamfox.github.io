All Out Assault On Maelstrom Hideout

Lukas Slavomíra 3:11 PM, February 02, 2080

Ordinary day in northern Kabuki was disturbed by explosions and gunfire in a police assault on Maelstrom's Allfoods hideout as SWAT teams stormed in with aerodynes.

In broad daylight, at 2 PM February 2nd, the NCPD's SWAT teams cleared out the Allfoods plant with force finding illegal drug manufacturing equipment, netrunning gear and weapons. The plant is as of writing this article, an active scene as the investigators are still scanning the plant.

The Kabuki Allfoods plant has been abandoned since 2070, after the Unification War, when Arasaka took control of the Watson area. Conveniently large, abandoned, well-equipped and open for various manufacturing operations, the Maelstrom has been squatting there for years now. That is, until now.

The renowned SWAT leader of the assault, Captain Ramirez Riggs, had the following to say:

“Our perimeter policing approach is gaining traction. Months of surveillance have been put in, and our officers did fine work with the gathered intel. Furthermore, this assault was an important step in driving the Maelstrom away from the city and making Night City safer for everyone. As far as we know, all remaining Maelstrom hideouts are in Watson Northside."

No comments were given about the number of suspects detained, but the fact is: Kabuki residents can now sleep more comfortably knowing the plant is rid of Maelstrom activities.
